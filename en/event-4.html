Motorex Expo 2025

Motorex Expo 2025 at Damascus Fairgrounds

Under the patronage of the Syrian Ministry of Transport and organized by "Mohammad Bin Marwan Orfali Consultancy & Partners", Motorex Expo 2025 will launch on August 5th at the Damascus Fairgrounds, with broad participation from more than 37 local, regional, and international companies from Syria, Jordan, the United Arab Emirates, and Germany. The exhibition will continue until the 9th of the same month. The exhibition aims to provide a leading platform that brings together major brands in the automotive, motorcycle, and heavy machinery industries and their accessories under one roof. This allows the public and enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements in transport technologies and learn about innovative solutions in the fields of cars, spare parts, and technical services, while also supporting economic activity and strengthening the automotive sector locally and regionally, according to the organizer Mohammad Orfali. Orfali stated that Motorex Expo is the first international specialized exhibition in Syriaâ€™s renewed automotive and machinery sector, representing both a symbolic and economic milestone that reflects the revival of one of the industries most affected by years of conflict. The exhibition will also feature two major special auctions, kart racing experiences, presentations on the latest electric vehicle technologies, installment purchase offers, hospitality services for visitors, as well as hourly prize draws. The exhibition will welcome visitors daily from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Attendance is open to car and motorcycle enthusiasts, machinery specialists, traders, businesspeople, and investors, providing an opportunity to enhance cooperation between national, regional, and international companies and contribute to revitalizing investment in the automotive and equipment sector.
