About the Guide

Damascus City Guide is a comprehensive online platform aiming to connect the Syrian community with various cultural, sports, music, and family events happening across Damascus. We strive to make discovering local events easy and to encourage community participation in cultural and recreational activities.

Through our platform, users can browse upcoming events, search for specific events by category, date, or location, and access detailed information about each event.

Publishing Policies

Event Acceptance Criteria:

  • The event must be open to the public or clearly targeted to a specific group
  • Complete information about the event must be provided (date, location, description)
  • The event must be legal and respect societal values
  • We reserve the right to review, edit, or reject any event before publishing

How to Submit an Event:

Event organizers can contact us via the "Contact Us" page to provide details about their events. Our team will review the request and respond within 48 hours.

Privacy Policy:

We respect our users' privacy and do not share their personal information with third parties. All collected data is used only to improve user experience and provide better content.
